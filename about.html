Get to know me

I am Zadkiel, and I live in sunny Orlando, Florida. By day, I am navigating the vibrant city life, but come nightfall, you will find me immersed in the thrilling worlds of video games. There is nothing quite like the excitement of a well-crafted virtual adventure or the challenge of a strategic gaming session.

When I am not playing video games, you can find me eating everything and anything. Orlando's diverse food scene is my playground. Whether savoring local favorites or discovering hidden gems, I am always up for a delicious adventure. From mouth-watering Korean BBQ restaurants to spots serving exotic flavors, food is a passion that adds spice to my life.
